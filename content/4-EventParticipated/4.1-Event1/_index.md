# Detailed Report: FCAJ Community Day - May 09, 2026

* **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day - May 09, 2026
* **Date & Time:** May 09, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
* **Role:** Attendee

---

### Description of Content and Main Activities

This community-driven IT workshop hosted by the AWS Study Group focused on career orientation, advanced prompt design, and automated software architecture using AI. The key sessions included:

1. **Learning Motivation & Brain Hacking:**
   - Explaining the brain's dopamine reward loop and how to "hack" it to enjoy learning.
   - Breaking down complex engineering tasks into micro-goals to prevent overwhelm.
   - Designing tiny reward systems for oneself to sustain long-term focus.
2. **Advanced Prompt Engineering:**
   - Understanding LLM mechanics: tokens, context windows, and structural prompting.
   - Designing precise prompt frameworks to minimize AI hallucination.
   - Practical demonstration of hosting a personal AI project on AWS infrastructure.
3. **Career Mindset & Advice for Fresh Graduates (Speaker: Khang):**
   - The philosophy that AI is an amplifier, not a replacement for fundamental engineering skills.
   - Focus on solid foundations: data structures, networking, OS, and security.
   - The value of professional integrity and asking the "Why?" behind architecture decisions.
4. **The BMX Method in Software Development (Speaker: Thao):**
   - Mitigating AI code generation failures from large, monolithic prompts.
   - Splitting the development process into agentic roles: Product Manager, Architect, Developer, and Tester.
   - Driving step-by-step code generation with structured documentation.

---

### Outcomes and Value Gained

* **Lessons Learned:** I gained a deep understanding of structured prompt design and the importance of understanding the "Why" behind technical decisions. I realized that AI should only serve to amplify existing engineering skills rather than replacing core computer science fundamentals.
* **New Skills:** Learned to apply the BMX method to divide large, complex software requirements into structured role-based prompts for more reliable code generation.
* **Career Application:** Setting up daily micro-goals for AWS certification learning path to establish a steady dopamine loop and maintain long-term learning habits.
