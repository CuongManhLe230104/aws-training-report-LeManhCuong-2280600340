During my internship, I participated in three events. Each one was a memorable experience that provided new, interesting, and useful knowledge, along with gifts and wonderful moments.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** May 09, 2026  

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee 

&emsp;**Description:** This community-driven IT workshop covered four key sessions: "Learning Motivation" (how to hack the brain's dopamine reward mechanism to enjoy learning like social media or gaming), "Prompt Engineering Techniques" (mastering tokens, clear prompt structures to prevent hallucinations, AI reasoning mechanisms, and hosting personal projects on AWS), "Career Advice for Fresh Graduates" (focusing on IT mindset, how AI amplifies rather than replaces core knowledge, the importance of foundational knowledge, asking 'Why', and professional integrity), and the "BMX Method in Software Development" (a structured approach to divide large AI code requests into defined roles like PM, Architect, Dev, and Tester for error-free software creation).  

&emsp;**Outcomes:** The event provided actionable insights on how to build persistent learning habits and leverage AI tools effectively without sacrificing core computer science fundamentals. The BMX method offered a solid model for structuring software projects, while the career advice reinforced the values of professional integrity and asking the right questions in software engineering.

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day - May 2026  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** May 30, 2026  

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Description:** This session explored strategies for AWS certified engineers to enhance their value through specialized skills. Topics included "Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci" (gamified learning and active practice methods), "Hackathon - A Ton of Fun and Bug Fixes" (the benefits and learning values of participating in hackathons), "Why We Always Need Confidence" (the importance of self-confidence and overcoming self-doubt in academic and professional life), "Tu vi Dai Viet" (applying AI to Vietnamese astrology/Bát tự and future web development plans), and "The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster" (understanding underlying DevOps practices to prevent system failure). The event also featured a valuable Q&A session with senior AWS professionals.

&emsp;**Outcomes:** The event reinforced the importance of continuous learning, self-confidence, and specializing in high-demand areas like AI and DevOps. Learning how to leverage gamified platforms, hackathons, and AI-powered applications to build a strong foundation in cloud engineering were key takeaways. It also highlighted the critical role of DevOps in modern software projects to avoid catastrophic failures.

### [Event 3](4.3-Event3/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day - June 2026  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** June 27, 2026  

&emsp;**Location:** Online on youtube channel AWS Study Group

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Description:** This online workshop focused on Cloud/AI career trends and enterprise AI agents. Key sessions included "Cloud/AI Engineer Career Journey" (Steve Tran sharing career insights and AI's impact on hiring), "Voice AI Agent for the Vietnamese Market" (Nghi, Kiet, and Trung explaining voice agent challenges in banking services), "DevOps AI Agent" (Bao and Nguyen introducing automated system troubleshooting), "Amazon Q for HR Management" (Truong and Minh Anh demoing CV screening automation), and "Securing Amazon Q with MCP Servers" (technical guide to connect Q to Jira/Zalo via VPC/MCP securely).  

&emsp;**Outcomes:** The event provided deep insights into enterprise-ready AI applications and their security frameworks (MCP, VPC). It highlighted how AI tools are transforming IT recruitment and operational tasks like DevOps troubleshooting and HR CV screening, emphasizing the transition from manual processing to AI-driven automation.
