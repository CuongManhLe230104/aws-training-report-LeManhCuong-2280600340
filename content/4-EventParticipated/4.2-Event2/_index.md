# Detailed Report: FCAJ Community Day - May 30, 2026

* **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day - May 2026
* **Date & Time:** May 30, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
* **Role:** Attendee

---

### Description of Content and Main Activities

This community event focused on sharing cloud methodologies, practical AI applications, DevOps fundamentals, and personal development. The main presentations included:

1. **Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci (Speaker: Huynh Thai Linh - Hutech):**
   - Utilizing AWS Cloud Quest (3D role-playing game) for hands-on, interactive cloud architecture simulations.
   - Using the Floci learning model to keep motivation and track continuous learning milestones.
2. **Benefits of Participating in Hackathons (Speakers: FPT Student Group):**
   - Sharing real-world experience of handling bug fixes, sleep deprivation, and team coordination under extreme time limits.
   - The value of building quick prototypes and pitching ideas to judges.
3. **Why We Always Need Confidence (Speaker: Nguyen Thi Quynh Nhu - Hutech):**
   - Discussing the balance between self-confidence and self-doubt ("sự tự tin - tự ti") in academic and professional career paths.
   - Actionable advice on how to build confidence and overcome impostor syndrome in interviews.
4. **Tu vi Dai Viet - AI-Powered Vietnamese Astrology Web Application (Speaker: Nghia Tran):**
   - Practical demonstration of "Tử vi Đại Việt", a web application applying generative AI to decode astrology and horoscopes.
   - Future plans to scale and host the web application on AWS services.
5. **The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster (Speaker: Tran Minh):**
   - Discussing the invisible foundation of software projects: CI/CD, testing, monitoring, and database rotation.
   - Best practices for setting up reliable automated operations early to prevent deployment disasters.

---

### Outcomes and Value Gained

* **Lessons Learned:** Gained insights into the practical uses of AI in traditional domains (astrology) and the invisible role of DevOps in software success. Understood the value of active, gamified learning for complex technologies.
* **New Skills:** Explored AWS Cloud Quest and understood how DevOps automation prevents systems from scaling failures.
* **Contribution to work:** Strengthened soft skills including self-confidence and teamwork, which are highly critical for developer collaboration in cloud engineering projects.
